DECENTRALIZED LOTTERIES

A collection of decentralized lottery Smart Contracts (SC) powered by the unstoppable DERO platform.

LOTTO COLLECTION

Browse a variety of DERO Lotto Smart Contracts. Remember, this is just the beginning...

Max Tickets Lottery

Buy a ticket to particate in the upcomming draw. The draw starts and reset when all the tickets are bought. Choose between multiple types of lottery with different parameters. There is only one winner per draw and the house gets a cut.

Time Lottery

Same as max tickets lottery with the exception that the lottery is drawn based on time, regardless of how many tickets are sold. For instance: daily, weekly or monthly draws.

Custom Lottery

Create your own lottery with the choice of multiple variables. Find the lottery that peaks your interest and buy a ticket.
